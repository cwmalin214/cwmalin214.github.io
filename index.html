Home Page for Connor Malin

I am a graduating fifth year Ph.D. student in the Math Department at the University of Notre Dame under the supervision of Mark Behrens. I research interactions between homotopy theory and geometry. I like to think about these problems through the lens of functor calculus and Koszul duality. I currently think about the relation between Goodwilie calculus, embedding calculus, orthogonal calculus, and factorization homology. In July, I will be starting a postdoc at the Max Planck Institute in Bonn.

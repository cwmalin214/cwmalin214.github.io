Home Page for Connor Malin

I am a fifth year Ph.D. student in the Math Department at the University of Notre Dame under the supervision of Mark Behrens. I research interactions between homotopy theory and manifolds. I am especially interested in the interactions of different variations of homotopy calculus. I enjoy finding explicit connections between Goodwillie calculus, embedding calculus, and factorization (co)homology. I have also recently started thinking about stable orthogonal calculus.

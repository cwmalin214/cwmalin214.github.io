Home Page for Connor Malin

I am a fourth year Ph.D. student in the Math Department at the University of Notre Dame under the supervision of Mark Behrens. I research interactions between homotopy theory and manifolds. I am especially interested in the interaction of Goodwillie and embedding calculus and what this can tell us about manifolds. I currently run the Notre Dame Graduate Topology Seminar with Lorenzo Riva.
